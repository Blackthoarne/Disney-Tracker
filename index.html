Dole Whip Finder

Curated
🏰 Magic Kingdom
Aloha Isle
Adventureland, next to the Enchanted Tiki Room / Magic Carpets of Aladdin
The original spot. Pineapple, vanilla swirl, Pineapple Float, Pineapple Upside-Down Cake. Mobile order often disabled — plan to walk up.
Sunshine Tree Terrace
Adventureland, near the hub
Orange, sometimes Strawberry, plus floats. Mobile order usually works here — good backup if Aloha Isle's line is long.

Refreshment Outpost
World Showcase, between China and Germany
Cherry-Pineapple Swirl, classic pineapple.
Swirled Showcase
Near Port of Entry, World Celebration/World Showcase
Rotating swirl flavors — changes with whichever festival is running.

Tamu Tamu Refreshments
Harambe Market
Pineapple. Reviewers consistently flag this as the weakest location resort-wide — melts faster, less fresh-tasting than other spots.
🎬 Hollywood Studios
No dedicated location

Swirls on the Water
The Landing
Deepest flavor bench anywhere on property: Pineapple, Vanilla, Strawberry, Raspberry, Lime, Lemon, Peach, Watermelon, Mango. Offers a 3-flavor Flight, a Sampler, Dark Chocolate Cone, and rotating seasonal sundaes.
Wine Bar George
The Landing
Frozcato — a Dole Whip-based boozy specialty.
🌊 Water Parks
Snack Shack
Typhoon Lagoon
Pineapple, Strawberry, Vanilla, and Green Matcha tea. Both alcoholic and non-alcoholic versions.
Warming Hut
Blizzard Beach
Orange, vanilla swirl.
🏨 Resorts
Pineapple Lanai
Disney's Polynesian Village Resort, near the lobby beneath 'Ohana
Classic pineapple. Barefoot Pool Bar and Oasis Pool Bar (also at the Poly) do boozy versions.
Fuel
Swan & Dolphin Resort Complex
6-flavor frozen yogurt/Dole Whip bar: Pineapple, Chocolate, Vanilla, Strawberry, New York Cheesecake, Caramel Corn — mix 1-2 flavors with 10 toppings.
